How It Works

I’m here to help you achieve your financial goals and simplify your bookkeeping. Whether you’re a small business owner, freelancer, or just trying to create a personal budget for some extra money at the end of the month, the process is easy.

Connect

If you’re using an accounting software, then I just need to login and get to work. Otherwise, I will consolidate your information in an Excel Spreadsheet to track your finances and generate monthly statements.

Share

To ensure your books are accurate, I’ll need some documentation to complete bank reconciliations and possible deductions. Don’t stress, you’ll have your own encrypted folder on Dropbox for maximum security.

Grow

I’ll deliver your completed finances along with a summary report at the beginning of every week so you can carry on with confidence. I’ll also be available anytime to answer any questions you may have. You can reach me by phone, text, or email.

Now you can focus on growing your business or throwing that party you’ve been planning.
